# Crystal F Biography

## Personal Information
- **Real Name**: Hauke Schmidt

## Career
Crystal F is a German rapper and member of the hip-hop group Ruffiction, alongside Arbok 48 and Crack Claus. He is the owner of the independent label Ruffiction Productions and has released multiple solo albums.

- **Started**: Professional music in 2004
- **Background**: Grew up in Estorf bei Nienburg
- **Current Residence**: Berlin, Germany

## Musical Style
- **Genre**: Hip Hop
- **Themes**: Often controversial, underground rap with themes of violence, drugs, and social issues
- **Production**: Known for working with various producers and collaborators in the German hip-hop scene
